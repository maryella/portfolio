About Me

Before web development, I spent several years as a pharmacist working in the retail setting. After a few online classes, I realized I really enjoyed coding and using code to solve problems. I decided to take the leap and enroll in an immersive bootcamp. It's challenging and a little scary, but I love it!

When I'm not coding, I enjoy traveling. Some of my favorite places are Montreal, Berlin, and Mexico City. While in town, you can find me going out to check out different restaurants or staying home and baking. Either way, I always like to try something new!
